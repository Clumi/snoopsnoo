About

{{ snoopsnoo_macros.logo_small() }} provides reddit user and subreddits analytics.

User analytics are computed by analyzing submissions and comments activity. Analysis is limited to the 1,000 most recent comments and submissions due to reddit's API restrictions.

Subreddits are automatically assigned topics by an algorithm. Subreddits with fewer than 1,000 subscribers or created within the last 30 days may not have been processed.

Feedback, criticism and suggestions are welcome and greatly appreciated - please PM /u/orionmelt.

DISCLAIMER: All data analyzed is public. This website is not affiliated with reddit and cannot access your private reddit data such as your password, subreddit subscriptions, up/downvotes and private subreddit activity.
