About

SNOOP SNOO is a hobby project by /u/orionmelt and an attempt to extract interesting information from your reddit comments and submissions.

DISCLAIMER: All data analyzed is public. This website is not affiliated with reddit and cannot access your private reddit data such as your password, subreddit subscriptions, up/downvotes and private subreddit activity.

Analysis is limited to the 1000 most recent comments and submissions due to reddit's API restrictions.

Feedback, criticism and suggestions are welcome and greatly appreciated - please PM /u/orionmelt.
